About this app

Procreate is the most powerful and intuitive digital illustration app available for iPad. Loved by creative professionals and aspiring artists, it offers hundreds of handmade brushes, a stunning collection of tools, an advanced layer system, and the revolutionary Valkyrie graphics engine.


Features:
• Ultra High Definition canvases up to 16k by 8k
• 200+ handmade brushes
• Full layer system with blend modes
• Color Harmony and Dynamics
• Time-lapse recording
• Import/export PSD files
